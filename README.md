Nimma-Guru 📚🌱

Community Mentorship App for Villages

Nimma-Guru is an Android application designed to connect retired teachers, skilled professionals, and experienced mentors with students in rural and local communities. The platform promotes free community mentoring, knowledge sharing, and quality education accessibility.


---

📌 Problem Statement

Many retired teachers and professionals are willing to share their knowledge and guide students, but there is no proper platform to connect them with students who need academic help and mentorship.

Nimma-Guru solves this problem by creating a simple and accessible mentorship platform for villages and local communities.


---

🚀 Features

👨‍🏫 Mentor registration and profile management

🎓 Student registration and login

🔍 Search mentors by subject and location

📅 View class schedules and mentoring sessions

💬 Appreciation and feedback messages

📱 Simple and user-friendly Android interface

🌍 Supports community learning and active aging
